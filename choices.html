果霸（Gobuster）用户隐私选择

最近更新：2025-08-17 ｜ 隐私政策 ｜ English

如何撤回“提交为改进样例”

  • 在应用内关闭“提交为改进样例”设置。
  • 如需删除已提交样例，请发送邮件至 3098848445@qq.com，标题“删除样例+设备信息（可选）”，我们将在 7 个工作日内处理。

如何申请删除数据

  • 邮件标题“删除数据申请+设备ID（可选）”，正文描述需删除的数据范围。
  • 我们将在核验后 7 个工作日内完成处理并邮件反馈。

如何关闭权限

  • iOS 设置 → 隐私与安全 → 相机/照片 → 关闭“果霸”。

Gobuster — Your Privacy Choices

Last updated: 2025-08-17 ｜ Privacy Policy ｜ 中文

Withdraw sample submission

  • Disable “Submit samples for improvement” in the app settings.
  • To delete previously submitted samples, email 3098848445@qq.com with subject “Delete samples + device info (optional)”. We will process within 7 business days.

Request data deletion

  • Email subject “Data deletion request + device ID (optional)”, and describe the scope.
  • We will process within 7 business days after verification and reply by email.

Revoke permissions

  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera/Photos → toggle off for “Gobuster”.
